Celebrating Love: Highlights from the National Wedding

Published on: April 28, 2025

The National Wedding was a breathtaking celebration of unity, culture, and tradition. Held at the heart of the capital, the event brought together families, dignitaries, and citizens from across the country to witness a symbolic ceremony that honored our rich heritage.

From vibrant traditional attire to heartfelt vows, the day was filled with moments of joy and pride. Performances by local artists, moving speeches, and the ceremonial exchange of gifts showcased the deep respect for customs that define our national identity.

As the festivities continued into the evening, the spirit of togetherness was undeniable. The National Wedding was not just a celebration of two individuals but a reminder of the values that bind us all — love, respect, and community.

Elon Musk Launches Starlink Services in Lesotho

Published on: April 15, 2024

In a groundbreaking move for connectivity in southern Africa, Elon Musk officially launched Starlink services in Lesotho. The satellite-based internet service promises to deliver high-speed broadband to even the most remote areas of the country, marking a significant step toward bridging the digital divide.

The launch event, attended by government officials, tech leaders, and local communities, highlighted the potential impact of Starlink on education, healthcare, and business development in Lesotho. With its low-orbit satellite network, Starlink aims to offer faster, more reliable internet access compared to traditional providers.

As installations begin nationwide, many citizens and businesses are optimistic about the new opportunities that improved internet access will bring. This marks a historic moment not only for Lesotho’s digital landscape but also for the broader vision of global connectivity championed by Musk and his team.
